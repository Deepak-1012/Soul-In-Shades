About

Welcome to Soul In Shades! I’m Deepak, and this is my first startup born from a deep passion for pencil art. I create hand-drawn pencil sketch portraits on A4-size sheets, crafted with care and emotion. Once completed, your portrait will be safely delivered to your doorstep. Whether it’s a birthday, wedding, anniversary, or any special occasion, a personalized pencil sketch is a heartfelt way to surprise your loved ones and make memories last forever. ✨
